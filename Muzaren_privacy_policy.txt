## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted by our service providers:
- **Database:** Neon (serverless PostgreSQL) — hosted on secure cloud infrastructure
- **Images:** Cloudinary CDN — globally distributed content delivery network
- **Cache:** Upstash (serverless Redis) — encrypted at rest and in transit

### 5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption in transit:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Encryption at rest:** Sensitive data including passwords and tokens are encrypted in our database
- **Password hashing:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with strong salt rounds — we cannot retrieve your plain-text password
- **JWT tokens:** Authentication uses short-lived JSON Web Tokens (15-minute expiry) with secure refresh token rotation
- **Secure storage:** Your access tokens are stored in your device's secure enclave (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) via flutter_secure_storage
- **Rate limiting:** We implement rate limiting on all API endpoints to prevent abuse
- **Access controls:** Employee access to user data is strictly limited on a need-to-know basis

### 5.3 Verification Documents

Government-issued identity documents submitted for verification are treated with the highest level of security:
- Stored in a private, access-restricted folder on Cloudinary
- Not publicly accessible via any URL
- Only accessible by authorised MuzaRen administrators during the verification review process
- Never shared with other users under any circumstances

### 5.4 Security Limitations

Despite our security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at [INSERT SUPPORT EMAIL].
